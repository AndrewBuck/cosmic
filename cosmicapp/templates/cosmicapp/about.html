Cosmos

Cosmic

Cosmic is a citizen-science project for Astronomy. The primary goal of the Cosmic project is to make the scientific tools of astronomy easy enough to access and use that someone without formal training may become a citizen of Cosmic, and make a meaningful contribution to humanity's scientific understanding of the Cosmos.

Citizens

The citizens of Cosmic are generators, analysers, and users of scientifically valuable astronomical data. Cosmic is built to simplify this process as much as possible.

Science

Cosmic citizens are scientists: professional, hobbyist, student, expert, and neophyte. Cosmic provides for a person interested in science, space, astronomy or a love of looking at the night sky a way to directly contribute to advancing humanity's understanding of the Cosmos.
